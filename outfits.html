Automated Personalised Outfits

97-99% of fashion retailers’ visitors leave a site without buying anything. Our outfits are generated in real-time and ensure that when a customer looks at a garment they see how they could wear it with new clothes and clothes they already own. This gives them the confidence to make the purchase turning browsers into buyers.

The Benefits

Showing Versatility

Women want to understand what to wear with an item and how it could work with clothes they already own. The Outfitter does just this, transforming what was previously a simple white shirt into a wardrobe essential.

Validating a purchase

The Outfitter demonstrates how an item can be worn in several different ways. Discovering the versatility and potential of your product helps validate the purchase, giving your customers the confidence to click that all-important “buy” button.

Upselling other items

Displaying a variety of items that go perfectly with the original means a customer sees products they may not have previously noticed. This encourages further browsing behaviour, or even an unexpected purchase.

Features

Multiple Occasions

Why should an item only be worn for a one off event? Allow customers to see how to get the most out of their potential purchase by showing them how they can wear it across multiple occasions, be it for work, a wedding, or the weekend.

Similar Items

Show customers similar items to the one they were originally looking at to give a wider variety of options.

Past Purchases

Women often consider how an item will work with their existing wardrobe before making a decision. By providing us with your customer’s past purchase data we are able to suggest outfits based around items they already own.

Flexible Design

Place the widget anywhere on your product page - horizontal or vertical, with your brand colours, fonts and design. It also works on all devices, be it on mobile, tablet or desktop.
