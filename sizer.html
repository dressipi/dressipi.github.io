The Perfect Size Every Time

What could be better than ensuring your customer buys the right size every time they shop? With Dressipi’s Size Finder Widget you can provide perfect size recommendations to every one of your customers for each individual garment at point of purchase.

Fewer Returns

It’s no surprise that customers are less likely to return the item if it fits them properly. Not having the added inconvenience of making the return keeps your customer happy, and also keeps you happy.

Confident Shoppers

Providing the correct size for each customer gives them the confidence to make that final push to purchase whilst increasing the likelihood of shopping with you again.

It's a no-brainer

We know how important it is for the functionality on your site to feel perfectly on brand. That’s why we work closely with you to get the Size Finder Widget exactly how you’d like it.

Fast Setup

Simple to Use

We make the process as easy as possible by asking your customer what size they are across all garment categories in their favourite brand with no need to scan or measure. It couldn’t be simpler.

Past Purchases

Flexible Design
