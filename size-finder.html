The Perfect Size Every Time

What could be better than ensuring your customer buys the right size every time they shop? With Dressipi’s Size Finder you can provide perfect size recommendations to every one of your customers for each individual garment at point of purchase.

The Benefits

Fewer Returns

It’s no surprise that customers are less likely to return the item if it fits them properly. Not having the added inconvenience of making the return keeps your customer happy and maintains your margins without the added cost of processing the returns.

Confident Shoppers

Providing the correct size for each customer gives them the confidence to make that final push to purchase whilst increasing the likelihood of them shopping with you again.

Features

Fast Setup

We’ve done all the hard work for you, meaning that the Size Finder comes pre-packaged and ready to go. Scroll down for more details on the integration process.

Simple to Use

We make the process as easy as possible by asking your customer what size they are across all garment categories in their favourite brand with no need to scan or measure. It couldn’t be simpler.

Past Purchases

If we have a customer’s past purchase history this means they don’t need to go through any sign up process. They can edit their size information for more accurate results if preferred.

Flexible Design

We follow your lead – colour, font, copy and placement is up to you ensuring your customers get a seamless branded experience.
