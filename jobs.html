Software Engineer

Working at Dressipi

Dressipi is an award-winning, mature start-up known for changing the way women buy clothes and make their dressing decisions. Since our launch in 2011, millions of women and many leading fashion retailers use our personalisation technology to find the right products that their customers will love.

We’re a small team making a big impact and are hugely passionate about leading the way in ‘true personalisation’. We are driven by putting the customer need at the heart of our solution and helping retailers genuinely embrace a one-on-one relationship with their shoppers.

About the Role

You’ll chiefly be working on the apis and backend technology that support these customer facing applications. You’ll also be supporting the front end developers who consume these apis – experience with front-end technologies such as JavaScript/Backbone/React is a bonus.

The majority of our work takes place on a set of rails apps (currently all on rails 4.2/rails 4.1 & ruby 2.2), with data stored in Elasticsearch, MySQL and MongoDB and hosted on AWS. A recent task you might have tackled was estimating a customer’s weight based on the other signup data they provide for a client that didn’t want to ask customers their weight.

In return, we offer an inspiring and collaborative environment and the opportunity to work alongside a world class team on one of the most ground-breaking personalisation solutions. We are tackling complex, data-related problems with some of the most diverse data sets, including contextual and emotional data as well as clothing feature analysis and user behaviour. We’re very lucky to have exceptional people and we place a high value on learning and personal growth.

What we're looking for

  1. Someone with an interest in solving hard and diverse problems
  2. The will and ability to pick up new technologies as required
  3. Interest in building web applications capable of servicing millions of users
  4. Good working knowledge of at least some of the technologies we use
  5. Full of positive energy and relish the thought of being part of a small, fast-moving team

Benefits

  1. A competitive salary and a generous equity stake in the company (we believe everyone should share in the success of the company)
  2. Building an exciting product that millions of people are using and appreciating everyday
  3. Working with awesome technologies (Rails, AWS, Elasticsearch etc.)
  4. Being part of one of the top technical teams in the UK
  5. Relaxed, sociable work environment with freedom and independence

Sounds like you?

Please send examples of previous work (websites, apps, Stack Overflow and Github handles) alongside your CV and any other info to jobs@dressipi.com
