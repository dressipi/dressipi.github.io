Her very own personal stylist

Dressipi’s Style Adviser service allows women to discover clothes and accessories that fit their shape, and perfectly suit their style and personal preferences. This means true personalisation and a dynamic service that can evolve with the customer as well as fashion.

The Benefits

Expert Stylist Advice

You’re in safe hands – we have some of the industry’s most renowned stylists working alongside our world-leading tech team to ensure your customers see the best possible edit of your products tailored to their shape and personal style.

Truly Personalised

We capture 90% of a woman’s fashion preferences, including context and emotion. This means we are able to offer unrivalled size, product and outfit recommendations completely personalised to each customer.

Proven Results

Through our retailer partnerships we are proven to deliver core KPI benefits and increased intelligence, as well as double digit increases to the incremental net revenue per customer by increasing basket size and reducing return rates.

Component Parts

Recommendations

We provide a personal edit of your current stock to each customer, showing them the products they are most likely to love and keep. This can be integrated into your existing category and new in pages or can exist as separate pages within the site.

Style Guide

A handy reference guide helping your customers understand what styles, necklines, shapes and sleeves are best for them and their body shape. This is typically linked back to relevant stock for easy purchasing.

Outfits

Women typically like to understand what to wear with an item across multiple occasions and how it could work with clothes they already own. We provide the ability to do just this, transforming what was previously a simple white shirt into a wardrobe essential.

Sizing

We provide perfect size recommendations to every one of your customers for each individual garment at point of purchase. Not only does this give them the confidence to make that purchase but it ultimately reduces the risk of them having to make a return.

Emails

Seasonal and weekly personalised emails boost engagement, loyalty and revenue per customer, delivering a rich customer experience that ensures emails are seen as a service and not as spam. Our partners see an average open rate of over 50% and equally compelling conversion rates.

Multichannel

The Style Adviser components currently work online, on mobile and instore.

Simple Integration

Style Adviser is simple to integrate – we require a full product feed and a set of designs based on a creative kit we provide. Past purchase history will improve our recommendations. Along with flat-fee pricing, the whole process of integration onto your site is easy and affordable.

  1. You create a page to host an iframe pointing at our services
  2. We build a custom application based on your designs and tailored to your requirements
  3. Accounts may be standalone or integrated with your customer accounts
  4. Personalised email data can be provided in any format to suit your email provider
  5. Product page outfits or size recommendations require additional integration

Analytics integrated

Style Adviser can be integrated with any existing retailer analytics to measure the impact on business.
