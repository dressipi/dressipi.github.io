Automated Personalised Outfits

97-99% of retailer’s potential customers leave a site without buying anything. Our automated outfit recommendations reduce this risk by tailoring to customer’s personal preferences, generated in real-time as they shop.

Context

Women want to understand what to wear with an item and how it could work with clothes they already own. The Outfitter does just this, transforming what was previously a simple white shirt into a wardrobe essential.

Versatility

The Outfitter demonstrates how an item can be worn in several different ways. By discovering the versatility and potential of your product, your customers now have the confidence to click that all important “buy” button.

Upselling

Displaying a variety of items that go perfectly with the original means a customer sees products they may not have previously noticed. This encourages further browsing behaviour, and even an unexpected purchase.
